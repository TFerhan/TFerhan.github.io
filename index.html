I am a final-year Data Engineering student at ENSA Khouribga, Morocco, passionate about building intelligent systems that bridge AI, cloud computing, and real-world impact. Currently, I am a Machine Learning Intern at Groupe Label’Vie (Carrefour), where I fine-tuned SegFormer-B4 to classify urban structures from satellite imagery and support data-driven retail expansion strategies. Previously, I interned at the Digital Development Agency of Morocco where I developed a custom AI chatbot for OpenDataGov using CamemBERT, SentenceTransformers, FAISS, and a full RAG pipeline with FastAPI and Drupal. I’ve also led independent research projects such as a real-time portfolio optimization engine (Kafka, Flink, MongoDB, TimescaleDB, Spring Boot, Grafana) and a Monte Carlo–based credit & market risk engine using Apache Spark and ML models on Databricks. Certified in AWS Cloud/Data Engineering and experienced across Python, Java, FastAPI, Spark, Docker, and Terraform — I aim to build AI-first infrastructure with strong data pipelines and minimal latency.
